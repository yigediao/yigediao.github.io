About

My research focuses on vision-driven closed-loop robotic systems for real-world manipulation and assembly, with an emphasis on construction-scale tasks using large industrial robots. I develop and validate 3D perception algorithms based on RGB-D sensing and point cloud geometry, and tightly integrate perception, state estimation, decision-making, and execution to enable robots to operate under geometric uncertainty, occlusion, and environmental variability. My work combines high-fidelity digital twins and real robotic workstations, ensuring that methods transfer reliably from simulation to physical deployment.

Research Interests

  • Closed-loop visual servoing and adaptive control for precision-critical tasks
  • Robotic assembly under uncertainty, addressing symmetry, tolerances, and imperfect components
